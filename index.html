- About Me -

Hello! i'm Jessica Prieto. I graduated from University of Alberta with a degree in Computer Engineering. I am passionate at creating mobile applications as well as web applications.

MOBILE DEVELOPMENT

I really enjoy creating Android apps! I really have gotten the hang of working with Android Studio. Some of my next goals in mobile dev would be creating a library to easily render views based on Material Design standards, and also expanding my mobile development knowledge to learning iOS dev as well as developing with React Native and other cross-platform solutions.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

My first ever dev experience was creating a simple website with HTTP and CSS. Then I started using different frameworks such as Jquery and Bootstrap. I enjoy creating a website from scratch and designing The UX/UI. Front-End development is easily my first love.

I also enjoy creating the backend services for sites. I have experience working with ASP.NET and was able to get a summer internship dealing with web apps, and I also experienced dealing with databases for the web.

COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT

In my time at the University of Alberta, I have gained very valuable leadership and communication skills through my volunteer position works.

  • President - Computer Engineering Club (2016-2017; 2017-2018): I lead a 19-member executive team which aims at connecting undergraduates with the faculty as well as the industry. As president, I was able to take part in organizing our yearly hackathons. In my time as president I was also able to improve our branding (online presence, merchandise) as well as change the organization structure for better communication and transitioning.
  • Computer Engineering Club - VP Social: I was incharge of any academic and social events of the club. Some of these events include: Meet A Computer Engineer - Panel night with experienced industry representatives, and also Trick or Treat - in collaboration with other discipline association in engineering
  • U of A - Orientation Leader (2014; 2015): A whole 2-day event where I introduced Frehmen Engineers to the facilities and serices on campus.

PAST-TIME ACTIVITIES

I love the outdoors! I go on yearly summer activities with my family which includes hiking, camping, and going on roadtrips wich each year topping the last. I also love going on vacation with my boyfiend an collecting memories with our video travel diaries. I'm also interested in doing a little bit of photography. I also liek singing and plating instruments. Another highlight of my undergrad was joining the yearly battle of the bands from my first year until my last.
